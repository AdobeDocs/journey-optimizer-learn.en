# Introduction to email decisioning

Discover how decisioning can transform email personalization for your campaigns. This guide focuses on using decisioning to ensure each recipient receives the most relevant and high-priority offers. For example, in the travel industry, decisioning can intelligently select and rank offers based on customer profiles, maximizing engagement and revenue.

The process involves creating a collection of offers, assigning eligibility rules, and applying ranking methods such as AI models, formulas, or static priority scores. Marketers can tailor the selection strategy to display the top offers effectively in emails. Testing ensures the strategy delivers accurate results.
