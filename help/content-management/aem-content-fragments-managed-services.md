# Use AEM Content Fragments (Managed Services) in Journey Optimizer

Learn how to configure and use Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) Managed Services integrations in Journey Optimizer to integrate content fragments into your authoring workflows.

This tutorial walks you through setting up repository connections, validating configurations with Content Advisor, and selecting content fragments directly within the personalization editor, enabling marketers to reuse and preview AEM content efficiently within their campaigns.
